Glossary

Akrah the idea of accountability, everyone will have to answer to Allah for their actions
Hadith

the traditions of the Prophet Muhammad
Haraam

forbidden or prohibited
Khalifa trustee or guardian
Qur’an the sacred text of Islam, revealed to the Prophet Muhammad
Shari’ah

Islamic law
Surah a section of the Qur’an
Tawhid central belief of Islam relating to the unity and oneness of Allah
Wudu ritual washing required before the 5 times of daily prayer
