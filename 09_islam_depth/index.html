Religious Tradition Depth Study 2 - Islam

This is one of 5 options available for Depth Studies of Religious Traditions in the Higher School Certificate Course. Studies of Religion I candidates must study 2 of the 5 Religious Traditions. Studies of Religion II candidates must study 3 of the 5 Religious Traditions.

22 indicative hours of study are allocated to each of the Religious Tradition Depth Studies.
