Islam: Significant People & Ideas

The focus of this study is the contribution of significant people and ideas to an understanding of Islam as a living religious tradition.

Syllabus page reference: 44-45.

Significant People and Ideas: Islam

Students learn about:

  • The contribution to Islam of ONE significant person OR school of thought:
    - Al Ghazali

  • The effect of that person OR school of thought on Islam

Students learn to:

  • Explain the contribution of ONE significant person OR school of thought to the development and expression of Islam:
    - Al Ghazali

  • Analyse the impact of this person OR school of thought on Islam
