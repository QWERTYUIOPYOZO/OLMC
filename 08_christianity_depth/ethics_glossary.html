Glossary

Anthropocentric

Human centred perception of creation

Eco Justice

Theology which highlights the common issues impacting on ecology and justice. Acknowledging that both issues stem from the same causes and require related solutions.

Ecology

Study of environment, study of world eco-systems

Feminist Theology

Branch of theology which affirms the status of women as equal to men

Kyoto Protocol

International agreement on reducing green house gas emissions with binding targets for industrialised nations. Australia has refused to ratify this protocol.

Messianic

To do with a coming Messiah. A messianic age is a future age when the Messiah will have come.

Pre industrialised

Refers to societies before the industrial revolution where there was no mass production of commodities.

Reign of God

Biblical notion of a future time, aspired to by Christians when the rule of God will be enacted - characterised by love, peace, justice etc.

Stewardship

Biblical notion of responsibility towards the environment.

Sustainability

Use of resources in such a way that they are able to regenerate and are not depleted.
