Glossary

Fundamentalist A member of a religious movement that is characterised by a literal, rigid interpretation of the sacred text.
Transcendent

Belief that a divine power/s or being/s beyond the human.

Utilitarianism Consequensialist form of ethical theory, which asserts the notion that what is good, is what brings the greatest happiness for the greatest number.
