Glossary

Abu Bakr first of the Four Rightly Guided Caliphs, ordered the preparation of the first written version of the Qur’an
Akhira life after death
Azra'il angel who receives the soul of a person at the point of their death
Calligraphy decorative writing style used by copyist of the Qur’an
Chain the line of transmission of a hadith
Hadith collections of traditions of the words and actions of the Prophet Muhamad
Hadith Qudsi sacred hadiths, believed to be revealed by Allah but communicated in the words of the Prophet
Hafiz a person who has memorised the entire Qur’an
Huffaz people who have memorised the entire Qur’an, plural of Hafiz
Iblis the devil, the chief jinn
Ibrahim biblical prophet (Abraham)
Isa biblical prophet (Jesus)
Kursi low stool on which the Qur’an is placed when being read
Musa biblical prophet (Moses)
Qur’an sacred text of Islam
Rusul messenger of Allah, prophet with a specific mission
Surah chapter of the Qur’an
Tawhid belief in the oneness and absoluteness of Allah
Zaid ibn Thabit prepared the first written version of the Qur’an
