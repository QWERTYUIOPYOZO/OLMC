Glossary

Al-'asr afternoon prayer
Al-'isha night prayer
Al-fajr dawn prayer
Al-maghrib prayer immediately after sunset
Al-zuhr midday prayer
Arkan al-islam the pillars of Islam
Dhul-Hijjah twelfth month of the Muslim calendar, the month of the Hajj
Hadith the collection of traditions of the words and actions of the Prophet Muhammad
Hajj the fifth pillar of Islam, the pilgrimage to Makkah
Hajjah one who has successfully performed the Hajj
Hajji one who has successfully performed the Hajj
Id-ul-Fitr feast marking the conclusion of the Ramadan fast
Iftar the food that is eaten to break the fast at the end of each day
Ihram attitude or disposition needed to undertake Hajj
Ka'bah sacred shrine in Makkah
Makkah the sacred city of Islam, in Saudi Arabia
Mosque Muslim place of worship
Niyyah intention
Qur'an the sacred text of Islam
Rak'ahs ritual actions and postures which accompany prayer
Ramadan the ninth month of the Muslim calendar, the month of fasting
Sa'y procession between Safa and Marwah
Sadaqah voluntary almsgiving or charitable work
Salat the second pillar of Islam, the ritual prayers
Sawm the fourth pillar of Islam, fasting
Shahada the first pillar of Islam, to bear witness
Talbiyah prayer prayed upon arrival in Makkah
Tarawih additional times of prayer during Ramadan
Tawaf encircling of the Ka’bah
Wuquf the standing before Allah at Mount Arafat
Zakat the third pillar of Islam, tithe to assist the poor of Islam, charitable tax
