Glossary

Akhira life after death
Al-Qadr destiny, the will of Allah
Angels messengers of Allah
Aqida five basic articles of belief of Islam
Azra'il angel who will be present at the side of the person dying to receive their soul as it leaves them
Barzakh place of waiting, where people go between death and the Day of Judgment
Dawud biblical prophet (David)
Iblis the devil, the chief jinn
Ibrahim biblical prophet (Abraham)
Immanent close to human beings, present in everything
Injil books revealed to Isa
Isa biblical prophet (Jesus)
Israfil angel whol has the role of calling all souls of the Day of Judgment
Jibril angel who revealed Qur'an to Muhammad
Jinn spirit figures
Mika'il angel who has the role of guarding places of worship
Munker angel who has the role of the questioner of souls
Musa biblical prophet (Moses)
Nabi an inspired messenger, prophet
Nadir angel who has the role of the questioner of souls
Nua biblical prophet (Noah)
Qur'an sacred text of Islam
Rusul an inspired messenger, prophet, given a specific mission
Sahifa scrolls revealed to Ibrahim
Shirk belief or action which diminishes faith in oneness of Allah
Surah chapter of the Qur'an
Tawhid Belief about the oneness and absoluteness of Allah
Tawrah books revealed to Musa
Transcendent beyond or outside of creation
Zabur books revealed to Dawud
