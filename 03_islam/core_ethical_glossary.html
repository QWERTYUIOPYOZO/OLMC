Glossary

Ayatollah recognised authority in Shi’ia community
Fard required action
Fiqh the process of Islamic jurisprudence
Hadith collection of the traditions of the Prophet Muhammad
Hadith qudsi sacred hadiths, believed to be the revelation of Allah expressed in the words of the Prophet Muhammad
Halal permitted action
Haraam forbidden action
Ijma ethical judgments of recognised Islamic scholarship
Ijtihad individual ethical judgments
Jurisprudence the collection of ethical judgments of Islam
Madhhabs schools of Islamic legal scholarship
Makruh hateful or undesirable action
Mandub desirable or recommended action
Mujtahid situation requiring prudent individual action
Mustahab recognised authority in Shi’ia community
Qiyas ethical judgments made through analogous application of the Qur’an
Qur’an sacred text of Islam
Shari’ah meaning “path” refers to the overall framework of ethical judgments
Sunna collection of the traditions of the Prophet Muhammad
Taqwa awareness or consciousness of Allah
Ummah a kind of kinship or family responsibility
Wajib required action
