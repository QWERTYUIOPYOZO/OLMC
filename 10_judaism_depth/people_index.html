Judaism: Significant People and Ideas

The focus of this study is the contribution of significant people and ideas to an understanding of Judaism as a living religious tradition.

Syllabus page reference: 46-47.

Significant People and Ideas: Judaism

Students learn about:

  • The contribution to Judaism of ONE significant person OR school of thought:
    - Moses Maimonides

  • The effect of that person OR school of thought on Judaism

Students learn to:

  • Explain the contribution of ONE significant person OR school of thought to the development and expression of Judaism:
    - Moses Maimonides

  • Analyse the impact of this person OR school of thought on Judaism
