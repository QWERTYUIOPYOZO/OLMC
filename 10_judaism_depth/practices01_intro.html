Approaches to Learning

The approaches suggested below indicate a range of strategies that may be undertaken to allow students to engage with the requirements of this part of the syllabus. Teachers may wish to use one or more of these approaches in addressing this area of study. The suggestions are intended as a guide only and are not exhaustive.

  • Visit a synagogue such as The Great Synagogue in Sydney

  • View a film of a Jewish ceremony; a Bar/Bat mitzvah or a wedding in a synagogue

  • Draw a floor plan of a synagogue, denoting the significant aspects.

  • Compare and contrast a floor plan of a synagogue with a floor plan of a Christian church, Mosque or a Hindu or Buddhist temple

  • Internet search for the functions of the synagogue
