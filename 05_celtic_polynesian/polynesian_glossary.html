Glossary

'Aho'eitu

The first Tu'i Tonga, born of the union between the god Tangaloa and the woman 'Ilaheva

Fale Fakataha

Ceremonial meeting house in Tongan society

Ha'apai

Volcanic Islands, part of the Tongan group

'Ilaheva

Mother of 'Aho'eitu the first Tu'i Tonga

Kava

Plant used to make kava drink, also name of the drink itself

Kava Ceremony

Young woman from the islet of 'Eueki sacrificed to the King whose grave became the source of the first kava plant

Mana

The divine power associated with ancestor heroes in Polynesian mythology

Maui

Polynesian god and ancestral hero

Niue

Polynesian island neighbouring the Tongan group of islands

Polynesian

Ethnic and cultural grouping of people from South Pacific islands such as Tonga and Samoa

Pulotu

Spirit world of Polynesian mythology

Tangaloa

Polynesian sky god, father of the first Tu'i Tonga

Tapu

Taboo or forbidden

Tawhaki

Polynesian god and ancestral hero

Tongatapu

Main island of the Tongan group where the capital Nuku'alofa is located

Tu'i Tonga

Honorific title held by the one considered to be the descendent of the first Tu'i Tonga, 'Aho'eitu, son of Tangaloa

Umu

Earth oven used in traditional Polynesian village life.

Vava'u

Part of the Tongan group of Islands
