Approaches to Learning

The approaches suggested below indicate a range of strategies that may be undertaken to allow students to engage with the requirements of this part of the syllabus. Teachers may wish to use one or more of these approaches in addressing this area of study. The suggestions are intended as a guide only and are not exhaustive.

  • Conduct an exegesis of selected biblical texts using reliable bible commentaries
  • Use a concordance to find examples of text which parallel those selected for this section of the course
  • Make a comparison of parallel passages from selected texts. Use a bible commentary to explain the different emphasis in the texts
  • Develop a mind map drawing together Bible references for one of the principal beliefs indicated below
