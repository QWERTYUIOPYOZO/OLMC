This area of study corresponds to aspects of the Preliminary Course Core Unit 6PC2 Jesus of History, Christ of Faith and the HSC Course Core Unit HSCC2 Spiritual Pathways

Through undertaking this area of study students will be able to achieve the following Unit Outcomes.

6PC2 Jesus of History, Christ of Faith

  • Give expression to personal understandings about the person and mission of Jesus
  • Describe and explain Jesus' response to the fundamental questions about the nature of the human person, human life and destiny
  • Identify and analyse the role Jesus plays in enabling others to respond to fundamental human questions
  • Study Gospel accounts highlighting the way Jesus challenged and confronted structures, authorities, people and values of his time
  • Critically reflect on the ways Christians today challenge and confront structures, authorities, people and values

6HSCC2 Spiritual Pathways

  • Describe significant experiences and people that have influenced their faith development and the expression of their spirituality
  • Research the scriptural and historical foundations of selected traditions of spirituality and their associated prayer forms
  • Analyse and critique contemporary expressions and trends in Australian society and religious communities related to people's search for authentic spiritual life
  • Critically reflect on and analyse the spirituality of biblical women in the Jewish and Christian traditions and of Muslim women
  • Reflect on their experience of participation in prayerful activities associated with various traditions of spirituality

Approaches to Learning

The approaches suggested below indicate a range of strategies that may be undertaken to allow students to engage with the requirements of this part of the syllabus. Teachers may wish to use one or more of these approaches in addressing this area of study. The suggestions are intended as a guide only and are not exhaustive.

  • Research the spirituality of the founder of the school or of a significant religious leader
  • Develop a personal prayer resource using a variety of prayer forms
  • Set a writing task to outline a variety of prayer forms
  • Complete a review of a published prayer resource
