Glossary

Constantine

Roman Emperor from the 4th century CE who converted to Christianity and began the process of Christianity becoming the state religion of the empire

Just War theory

Seven point theory testing the moral justification of engaging in war

Messianic age

Anticipated future age of peace and wellbeing following the return of the messiah

National Council of Churches of Australia

Organisation representing Christian denomination in Australia

Non-combatant

A person in a war zone who is not engaged in the fighting - a civilian.

Pacem in terries

Letter of Pope John XXIII on world peace

Pacificism

A state of non violence. A person who refuses to use violence is known as a pacifist

Pax Christi

Literally the peace of Christ. An international peace organisation

Peace of God

Also known as the peace of Christ. State of peace and wellbeing stemming from the presence and/or spirit of God

Society of St Columba

Catholic missionary order well known for their work in peace and peace education

Society of friends

Also known as Quakers. Christian denomination which maintains a pacifist stance

World Council of Churches

International ecumenical organisation representing national councils of Churches
